FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters):
Adaptalyfe empowers individuals with developmental disabilities to manage daily life independently. Our comprehensive app provides tools for task management, financial tracking, mood monitoring, and connecting with caregivers.

KEY FEATURES:
- Daily Task Management with rewards
- Financial tracking and bill reminders  
- Mood and symptom logging
- Caregiver communication tools
- Meal planning and shopping lists
- Appointment scheduling
- Medical information management
- Personal documents storage
- Sleep tracking
- Accessibility-focused design

Adaptalyfe is designed with accessibility in mind, featuring high contrast modes, large text options, and intuitive navigation. Our mission is to foster independence and improve quality of life.
